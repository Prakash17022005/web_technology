Delhi, the capital city of India, is a vibrant metropolis with a rich history and modern infrastructure. It is known for its historical landmarks like the Red Fort, India Gate, and Qutub Minar, alongside being the political hub of the country. Delhi is also famous for its diverse culture, bustling markets, and a blend of old-world charm with contemporary urban life. The city serves as a major center for politics, commerce, education, and culture in India.
